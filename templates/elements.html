There is plenty to do in the historic town of Queenscliff, one of the most popular tourist spots in Victoria.

The fine old buildings and monuments in and around the town are reminders of its romantic and colourful maritime past.

  • Queenscliff Music Festival, held the last weekend in November.
  • Great golf courses: Queenscliff Island Golf Course, Lonsdale Golf Course, Thirteenth Beach Golf Links, Barwon Heads Golf Course, Curlewis Golf Course, Portarlington Golf Course.
  • Fantastic walks and bike rides (Point Nepean National Park, ‘Walk it, jog it, ride it’ Rail Trail to name a couple).
  • Restored steam engine ride from Queenscliff to Drysdale, the Blues Train including dinner and the Portarlington Miniature Railway.
  • Historic Fort Queenscliff.
  • Wineries.
  • Fishing and diving charters and dolphin swims.
  • Local markets, shopping.
  • New Queenscliff Marina with cafés, shops and restaurants.
  • Museums, galleries and bookshop.
  • Queenscliff and Sorrento car and passenger ferry.
  • South Bay Eco Adventures.
  • Queenscliff Day Spa.
  • Family fun at Adventure Park, mini golf, Marine Discovery Centre.
  • Organised sightseeing tours and walks.
